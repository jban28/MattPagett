I'm an artist based in London, UK.
matthew.pagett@gmail.com

This website primarily focuses on the drawings I've been making over the past ten or so years. Whatever the subject, there's always a wider theme at the back of my mind: how do I, as someone compelled to make images with my hands, deal with the status and prevalence of the modern digital image - pixelated, ghostly, disposable etc?

I've been a bookseller, a digital TV/web designer, a freelance writer/illustrator, a hospital radio DJ and the cloakroom attendant at London's last-remaining leather bar. I currently work at Arthouse as an art instructor/designer and host a fortnightly, men-only life drawing session at a hair salon in Dalston, London.
